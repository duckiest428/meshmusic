Why switch

A music app that respects the music.

Most players treat your library like a queue. Mesh Player treats it like a collection.

Spatial audio, decoded properly

Dolby Atmos tracks are detected automatically and routed to Direct Multi-Channel output on compatible hardware — no manual switching, no guesswork on bitrate.

ATMOS

Synced lyrics, full screen

Words rise and fade in time with the track, set against a blurred wash of the album art — built for the moment you stop working and start listening.

Genres that actually sort

Browse by artist, album, or genre with instant filtering — thousands of tracks, zero lag, sorted the way you'd sort them by hand.

Favorite without friction

One tap on any row to mark a favorite. They collect automatically into a living playlist — no drag, no drop, no second app.

Point it at a folder, get a library

Choose any Music or Downloads folder and Mesh Player scans, tags, and shelves everything — artist, album, genre, format — in one pass. No importing wizard, no duplicates.
